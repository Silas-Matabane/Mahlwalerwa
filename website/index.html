About Us

A Proudly South African Company Built on Excellence

Mahlwalerwa (PTY) LTD was established in 2015 in Kempton Park, Ekurhuleni (CIPC Reg: 2015/397194/07) and is a Grade 4 CIBD-certified partner for construction and maintenance projects.

Led by our founder, Mr. Sepitle Matabane (BCom Hons Marketing), we serve households, businesses, and public-sector clients with dependable service and transparent delivery.

Beyond premium beds, furniture, and IT solutions, we deliver construction & renovations, engineering & repairs, facility management, cleaning & hygiene, events & catering, and skills development courses — all under one roof.
